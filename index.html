About

How I think about health & communication

01

Research Focus ▾

Communication patterns — especially self-silencing and help-seeking — among Korean American midlife women. How culturally specific styles interact with stress, coping, and access to care.

02

Methods ▾

Mixed methods (qualitative + quantitative) to bridge lived experience and measurable outcomes. Full-cycle: observation → theory → experimentation → field.

03

Intervention Philosophy ▾

Small shifts in how people interpret their circumstances can redirect self-defeating patterns into self-enhancing ones. Psychologically wise intervention design.

04

What's Next ▾

PhD program (Fall 2027) in health communication, developmental science, or educational psychology. Evidence to implementation.

How do we design messages that work with — not against — the cognitive and cultural contexts people actually inhabit?
