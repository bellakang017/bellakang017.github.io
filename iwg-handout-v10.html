Build Your HIVE
02 / 03
HIVE

Every bee in a hive has a role. In an interview, you do too.
Use your animal’s prompt to build a story interviewers remember.

H
Habitat
Set the scene. Where, when, what was happening?
I
Initiative
What was your role? Why did you step up?
V
Venture
What did you do? Be specific about your choices.
E
Effect
What happened? Numbers help.

1

Structure

🐝 Ask
Rewrite this into something sharp:
“How can we improve our company’s hiring process?”
🐝 Structure
Break this into MECE buckets (no gaps, no overlaps):
“Why are international grad students struggling to get US interviews?”
🐝 Refine
What would you improve about this AI answer?
“To increase sales, focus on social media, improve the website, and hire more salespeople.”
